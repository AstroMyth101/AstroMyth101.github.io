About

Engineering is where ideas meet wiring, tolerances, and inconvenient reality.

My work sits at the intersection of mechanical design, embedded firmware, controls, and pneumatic actuation. I enjoy building systems that move, sense, respond, and provide measurable feedback rather than remaining as clean diagrams on paper.

I value clear documentation, practical testing, and honest limitations. A design that works only in a slide deck is decoration; a design that survives troubleshooting earns respect.
